Indian sailfin dragon

The indian sailfin dragon is one of a big family of sailfin dragons collectifly named Hydrosaurus and look really cool.

Appearence

Indian sialfin dragons are the heaviest and largest of the sailfin family they share a close resembelence to comodo dragons but are generly smaller and have one big fin on there back. It has grey sandlike collord scales and have a yellow collord belly.

Habbitat

They are only found at the coast of india and other large satlwater bodies of water. They live on the rocks of the coast and hunt there for fish you can find them in mangroves too.

Food and Hunting behaviour

There able to run ontop of water for short distances with there large feet and tail as balance. They fish for food by diving down into the water or waiting for fish to strand onto the rocks.
